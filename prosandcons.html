Pros and Cons of Adopting Dogs and Cats

Dogs

Pros

  • They're loyal and often become "man's best friend".
  • Great for physical activity; they love walks!
  • They greet you enthusiastically when you get home.

Cons

  • Require regular physical activity, regardless of the weather.
  • They need training, especially as puppies.
  • Regular check-ups and preventive care are a must.

Cats

Pros

  • Generally more independent and can be left alone longer.
  • They're expert hunters and can help with pests.
  • Purring has a calming effect and can reduce stress.

Cons

  • Some can be aloof or picky about social interactions.
  • Litter boxes need to be cleaned regularly.
  • They love to climb and might knock things over.
